MESSAGE FROM OUR SHEPHERD

Words of wisdom and guidance from our spiritual leader

M/S/S/EVANG. ANTHONY THOMAS

SHEPHERD-IN-CHARGE & SPIRITUAL LEADER

Welcome to our church family. I am honored to serve as your shepherd and guide you on this beautiful journey of faith. For over 10 years, I have been blessed to minister to this wonderful community here in Edinburgh.

My heart's desire is to see each member of our congregation grow in their relationship with Jesus Christ, discover their God-given purpose, and experience the abundant life that comes from walking in faith.

A WORD FROM THE HEART

WALKING IN FAITH TOGETHER

Dear Beloved Congregation,

Grace and peace to you in the name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. As I reflect on our journey together as a church family, my heart is filled with overwhelming gratitude for the faithfulness of God and the dedication of each one of you.

In these times of change and uncertainty, we are reminded that our God remains the same - yesterday, today, and forever. He is our rock, our fortress, and our deliverer. As your shepherd, I want to encourage you to hold fast to the hope that we have in Christ Jesus.

The Celestial Church of Christ has always been a beacon of light in our community, and I believe God has great plans for our future. Together, we will continue to worship in spirit and truth, serve our neighbors with love, and proclaim the Good News to all who will hear.

OUR VISION FORWARD

As we move forward as Gold Parish, I envision a church that:

  • Worships with passion - Where every service is filled with genuine praise and adoration
  • Studies with purpose - Where God's Word transforms lives and guides decisions
  • Serves with love - Where we reach out to our community with Christ's compassion
  • Grows with grace - Where every member discovers and uses their spiritual gifts

I believe that with God's help and your partnership, we can achieve these goals and become an even greater blessing to Edinburgh and beyond.

PERSONAL INVITATION

Whether you are a longtime member or a first-time visitor, I want you to know that you are valued, loved, and have a place in this church family. If you ever need prayer, counsel, or just someone to listen, please don't hesitate to reach out to me.

My door is always open, and my prayer is that you will find in our church not just a place of worship, but a home where you can grow, serve, and experience the fullness of God's love.

May God's richest blessings be upon you and your family.

In Christian Love and Service,
M/S/S/Evang. Anthony Thomas
Shepherd-In-Charge, CCC Glory of Latter Days Parish
